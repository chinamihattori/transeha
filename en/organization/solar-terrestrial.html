ORGANIZATION

Solar-Terrestrial Environmental History

Estimated Impacts on Modern Civilization from Severe Solar Storms in Historical Documents

ISEEが名古屋大学デジタル人文社会科学研究推進センター(DHSS)と連携し、太陽黒点、日食、オーロラなどの歴史文献、考古資料や、宇宙線、太陽電波、地磁気などの各種アナログ記録を用いて、過去の激甚太陽嵐や太陽地球環境長期変動の復元を進めます。さらのその規模を最新の数理モデルから定量評価することで、今後発生する激甚太陽嵐や太陽地球環境長期変動による現代文明への影響を見積もることも目指します。

構成員

Hisashi HAYAKAWA

Institute for Space-Earth Environmental Research, Nagoya University
Division for Integrated Studies
Assistant Professor

Solar-Terrestrial Physics, Extreme Space Weather, Historical Astronomy, Environmental History

We are exploring extreme solar storms and long-term solar activity, using reports on sunspots, geomagnetisms, and aurorae in analog observational records and historical documents. On their basis, we are extending the chronological coverage of the solar storms and solar activity into centennial/millennial timescales to explore the extremity of the space weather and the space climate.

Jun'ichi SAKUMA

Center for Digital Humanities and Social Sciences, Nagoya University
Professor

Yukiko KAWAMOTO

Center for Digital Humanities and Social Sciences, Nagoya University. Graduate School of Humanities, Nagoya University. Institute for Advanced Research, Nagoya University. Research Center for Cultural Heritage and Texts, Nagoya University.
Associate Professor

Classics, Ancient History (Roman), Architectural History.

My primary research focuses on ancient Roman domestic sphere, especially houses and gardens in Italy. By combining classical literature (both Greek and Latin) and archaeological evidence, along with wood anatomy and C14 dating, I try to understand how ancient Romans lived within the household. I am also involved in research projects such as analysing astronomical events through classical sources, which has led me to participate in the Transdisciplinary Network linking Space-Earth Environmental Science, History, and Archaeology.

Naoya IWATA

Center for Digital Humanities and Social Sciences, Nagoya University
Associate Professor

Ancient Philosophy

Specializes in the epistemology and metaphysics of ancient philosophy, focusing on Plato and Aristotle. His primary research investigates foundational beliefs and methodologies for acquiring knowledge, contrasting empiricism with innatism. Recently, he has led the development of "Humanitext Antiqua," an AI-based chat system designed to analyze classical texts using large language models, aiming to innovate methods in Western classical studies and education.
